Facts:

  • Volunteerism has a value of over $184 billion dollars
  • Over 71% of volunteers work with only one organization each year.
  • 67% of people found volunteer opportunities online in 2014 vs 34% in 2006
  • Global estimates place the number of volunteers worldwide at 970 million by 2017.
  • The largest age group for volunteers was 35-44, the CNCS survey said, and volunteers were most likely to be parents with children under 18.
  • The top four volunteer areas are for religious (34.1%), educational (26%), social service (14.9%), and health (7.3%) organizations.
  • On average, people spend an average of 52 hours per year volunteering their time.
  • Analysis by the United Nations Volunteers (UNV) programme shows that the efforts of the more than 1 billion volunteers around the world are equal to that of 109 million full-time workers.
  • The vast majority of volunteer work, 70%, does not involve any organization but happens informally between people in their communities.
  • More than 90 countries around the world now have policies or legislation on volunteering by 2018.
